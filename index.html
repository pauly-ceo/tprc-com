About

An operating company in a hospitality real-estate wrapper. Personal resorts. Branded, built and operated end-to-end.

The Personal Resort Company defines a new category: the personal resort. The owner holds the asset. We operate it. Single-party accountability for both yield and lifestyle — there is no second number to chase, no second hand to shake.

We source the land, we design the building, we construct it, and we run it. That is the system. From a coastal plot in Bali to a residential cluster in Canggu, every property is delivered through one integrated stack and reports to one operator.

Backed by FIPOX. Built by THIERRA Construction. Operating across Bali, with Croatia coming online in 2027 and the head office in Czechia.

What we do

Three capabilities, one operator, one P&L per property.

01
Property development

Land sourcing, architectural design, regulatory navigation, construction oversight delivered with THIERRA Construction. Every site goes from due diligence to handover under a single project office.

02
Hospitality operations

End-to-end resort operation per property — bookings, housekeeping, F&B, distribution channels and on-site service. One brand standard, one operating system, executed locally.

03
Capital & partnerships

Owner–investor relationships, structured leasehold, transparent monthly reporting, and a partner sales network across two active markets and twenty in pipeline.
