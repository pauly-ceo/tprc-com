01 / Category owner

We don’t build for the market. We build the market.

TPRC is not another developer. We are the architect of a category that did not exist before us: places that fall between a private home and a hotel, owned by their guests, run as a hospitality system, financed as institutional real estate.

The brand the guest sees is its own — Ananta, Plava Uvala, Lesní stránê. TPRC stays behind, holding the structure together: development, capital, operations, reporting.

Earns respect. Voice of the builder · vision · system.
02 / The Manifesto
For most of history, a resort was something you visited. You arrived, you checked in, and eventually you left.
We build a new kind of place. Resorts people don’t just stay in, but belong to. Places that can be owned, experienced, and shared. Where your investment lives in something real.
A Personal Resort is part of a growing global system connected by a simple belief: ownership should feel personal.
Our owners aren’t distant investors watching numbers on a screen. They enjoy the resort themselves, welcome guests from around the world, and participate in the value the place creates.
Own a place in the world. Experience it personally. Share it with others. Benefit from what it becomes.

Welcome to the world of Personal Resorts.

Why we exist. The belief that holds the system together.

II. What we do

Three promises.
One operating system.

With us you can OWN the property, PROFIT from real yield, and EXPERIENCE world-class hospitality — all in the same place. That is what makes a resort personal.

01

Own

You own a real piece of real estate, not a token, not a fund unit. Title deed, your name, transferable and inheritable. Leasehold in Bali, freehold in the EU — structured to the local law, financed alongside FIPOX equity.

Your ownership is not a story on a screen — it’s in the register.

Real deed. Title in your name · CZ · EU · ID jurisdictions
02

Profit

Your unit runs as part of the Ananta hospitality operation. Bookings, guest service, cleaning, revenue management — we do it. You get the report and the distribution.

Net yields target 9 to 16 % per project. Statements arrive monthly, in your language, in your currency.

9–16 % target IRR. Monthly distributions · owner portal · transparent reporting
03

Experience

Every year, you get fourteen nights in your unit — yours, free of charge, on dates you choose. Plus the full hospitality stack: 24/7 villa manager, private chef on call, airport transfer, masseur, kids’ club, the lot.

That is the «personal» in Personal Resort. Not theoretical — in your calendar.

14 nights / year. Personal villa manager · private chef · full hospitality service
04

The System

We don’t outsource the hard parts. Construction runs through THIERRA, a builder we control end-to-end. Capital sits with FIPOX, our own group balance sheet — not third-party LPs. Operations are run by us, on the ground, in each destination.

Skin in the game at every layer. That’s why the numbers hold up.

Built by THIERRA.
Backed by FIPOX. Vertically integrated · in-house operations · skin in the game
